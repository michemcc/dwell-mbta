DWELL.

Real-time MBTA arrivals for Greater Boston. Live predictions for every subway, commuter rail, and bus stop — updated every 20 seconds, free, no account required.

MBTA Lines Covered

  • Red Line — Alewife to Braintree / Ashmont
  • Orange Line — Oak Grove to Forest Hills
  • Green Line — All branches: B (Boston College), C (Cleveland Circle), D (Riverside), E (Heath Street / Medford)
  • Blue Line — Wonderland to Bowdoin
  • Silver Line — SL1, SL2, SL3, SL4, SL5
  • Commuter Rail — All 13 lines including Providence, Fitchburg, Worcester, Newburyport/Rockport, and more
  • Bus — Local and express routes across Boston, Cambridge, Somerville, and surrounding towns

Features

  • Search any stop by name — Park Street, South Station, Salem, North Station, and more
  • Live departure countdowns updating every 20 seconds
  • Service alerts for delays, suspensions, and shuttle buses
  • Trip planner: find direct connections or 1-transfer journeys between any two stops
  • Save favourite stops for instant one-tap access
  • Live commuter rail train map showing real-time vehicle positions
  • Switch between lines at shared stations like Downtown Crossing and North Station

About DWELL

DWELL is an independent transit dashboard for Boston commuters, students, and visitors. All data comes from the official MBTA v3 real-time API. DWELL is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority.
